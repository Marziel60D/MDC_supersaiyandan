How to Play 2048 Super Saiyan Dan: Step-by-Step Guide

Objective:
The goal of this game is to reach the coveted "Super Saiyan Blue Kaioken" tile equals to score of 2048 by combining lower super saiyan state tiles on a grid, if you're strong enough you may continue playing after the goal to achieve the "True Ulra Instict" tile equals to score of 8192.

The Grid:
The game is played on a 4x4 grid. Each grid cell can contain a Super Saiyan state tile, you may check the tiles below this guide.

Starting Tiles:
At the beginning, two saiyan tiles with a value of "2" are placed randomly on the grid.

Swipe to Move:
Use arrow key for desktop/laptop devices, or swipe gestures for mobile devices (up, down, left, right) to move all tiles simultaneously in the desired direction.

How to Play 2048 Super Saiyan Dan: Step-by-Step Guide

Tile Movement:
When swiping, all tiles move as far as possible in the chosen direction, until they hit the edge of the grid or another tile.

Combining Tiles:
When two tiles with the same saiyan state or value collide after a move, they merge into a single tile with the higher saiyan state and the sum of their values. For example, two "saiyan" tiles with a value of "2" merge to form a single "false super saiyan" tile with a value of "4".

Scoring:
Points are earned each time tiles are combined. The value of the combined tile is added to the score.

Winning:
You win the game when you create a tile with the value of "2048".

How to Play 2048 Super Saiyan Dan: Step-by-Step Guide

Losing:
The game ends when the grid is full, and no more moves can be made. Keep an eye on available moves to prevent the grid from filling up completely.

Strategy Tips:
Focus on keeping the larger tiles in one corner to maximize space for further combinations. Try to anticipate the consequences of each move to avoid trapping high-value tiles in inconvenient positions.

Restart Option:
If you lose and you want to start over, just click any arrow key or swipe in any direction.

Enjoy the Challenge:
2048 Super Saiyan Dan is a game of strategy and patience. Experiment with different tactics and enjoy the challenge of reaching the elusive "2048" tile!

HOW TO PLAY: Use arrow keys for tile movement on computers, or swipe on mobile devices. When tiles with the same value touch, they merge into one. Aim to reach a score of 2048 to achieve "Super Saiyan Blue Kaioken". Keep going to reach the ultimate goal of "True Ultra Instinct" with a score of 8192.
